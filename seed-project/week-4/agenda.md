# Week 4 Agenda

Use this 60-minute club meeting structure for Week 4.
Split it into six 10-minute blocks.

| Minute | What to say | What to do |
|---|---|---|
| 0-10 | Freeze and safety check | Confirm what is frozen and what must not change |
| 10-20 | Team A rehearsal | Practice Team A's demo and speaking order |
| 20-30 | Team B rehearsal | Practice Team B's demo and speaking order |
| 30-40 | Fixes and final cleanup | Close the last bugs, docs gaps, and slide issues |
| 40-50 | Showcase setup | Check the room, devices, online voting link, and voting flow |
| 50-60 | Final recap | Confirm the event plan, archive handoff, and speaking roles |
