# Week 1 Agenda

Use this 60-minute club meeting structure for Week 1.
Split it into six 10-minute blocks.

| Minute | What to say | What to do |
|---|---|---|
| 0-10 | Welcome, goal, and club rules | Explain the meeting goal, attendance, and the 4-week season plan |
| 10-20 | Leader introductions | Candidates introduce themselves and explain their style |
| 20-30 | Team split and role choices | Form the two teams and pick role titles |
| 30-40 | Project brainstorming | Compare project ideas and choose the best one |
| 40-50 | Repo setup and roadmap | Create the repository, roadmap, and task board |
| 50-60 | Starter tasks and close | Assign beginner tasks, confirm owners, and end the meeting |
