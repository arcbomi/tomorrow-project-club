# Week 3 Agenda

Use this 60-minute club meeting structure for Week 3.
Split it into six 10-minute blocks.

| Minute | What to say | What to do |
|---|---|---|
| 0-10 | Goal and recap | Review what changed and what is still open |
| 10-20 | Team A progress | Show feature updates, fixes, and demo status |
| 20-30 | Team B progress | Show feature updates, fixes, and demo status |
| 30-40 | Testing and polish | Review bugs, UI fixes, and documentation progress |
| 40-50 | Decisions and priorities | Decide what gets finished before freeze |
| 50-60 | Next steps | Assign final Week 4 tasks and presentation work |
